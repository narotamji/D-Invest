Services

Insurance

Term Insurance – Ensures that the dependent family members do not suffer financially in the absence of main earning member. Don’t let sudden tragedy uproot your family. Even after you pass, there are still people who rely on you. It’s not for you – it’s for those you care most about.

Personal Accident & Disability Insurance – Ensures that the income doesn’t stop if the main earning member is disabled temporarily or permanently due to an accident.

Health Insurance – Ensures quality health care for you & your family. It also ensures that Medical Emergencies don’t turn into a Financial Crisis.

Critical Illness Insurance – Ensures a lump sum compensation if the person gets diagnosed with some critical illness.
